How to play Hearts

Goal

Avoid hearts (1 pt each) and the Queen of Spades (13 pts). Lowest score when someone hits 100 wins!

Passing cards each round

  • Round 1: pass 3 cards left →
  • Round 2: pass 3 cards ← right
  • Round 3: pass 3 cards ↔ across
  • Round 4: no passing (hold)
  • Cycle repeats

Playing tricks

  • Player with 2♣ leads the first trick
  • You must follow suit if you can
  • Hearts can't lead until "broken" (played on a trick)
  • No point cards on the very first trick
  • Highest card of led suit wins the trick

🌙 Shooting the moon

Take ALL 13 hearts + the Queen of Spades and everyone else gets +26 instead of you. Big risk, big reward!

Winning

Play until someone reaches 100 points. The player with the lowest score wins!
